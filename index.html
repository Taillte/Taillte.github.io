Taillte May

Taillte is a postdoctoral researcher at Instituto Superior Técnico in Lisbon, working on the physics of black holes and gravitational waves.

Black hole Nonlinearities

General Relativity is a nonlinear theory and we expect to see nonlinear effects when gravitational curvature is at its strongest -- in the vicinity of black holes.

  • Learn more

Black Hole Superradiance

Ultralight bosons can by amplified by scattering off black holes. This phenomena allows us to constrain the existence of these particles.

  • Learn more

Tests of Consistency with Black Holes of General Relativity

Gravitational waves allow us to probe the physics of regions where gravitational curvature is at its strongest. These signals may give us the first clues as to deviations between gravity in our universe and Einstein's General Relativity.
